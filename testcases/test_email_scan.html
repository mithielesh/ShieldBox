A deceased client left behind this money, and I need your help to transfer it out of the country. I am prepared to offer you 30% of the total amount for your assistance.

To proceed, I need you to send me your bank account details, including:
1. Full name
2. Bank account number

4. Address and phone number

Best regards,
Mr. Johnson Williams
